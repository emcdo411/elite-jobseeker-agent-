## Overview

The **Elite Jobseeker Agent** is a six-phase career intelligence framework engineered for Director-level and above job seekers targeting $120K-$200K+ roles in enterprise technology, consulting, financial services, and operations.

It does not summarize your resume. It does not tell you what you want to hear. It diagnoses your market position, rebuilds your profile for passive candidate positioning, audits cross-platform consistency, and generates Director-register outreach and negotiation language -- all from the recruiter's side of the screen.

Built on Jabez Ivan Joshiraj's 4-Step Framework. Director Intelligence Layer by Erwin Maurice McDonald, Epoch Frameworks LLC.

## What's New in v2.0

[![Breaking Change](https://img.shields.io/badge/v2.0-Breaking_Improvements-2563EB?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)

v1.0 got you to the resume. v2.0 gets you to the offer table.

| # | Improvement | v1.0 | v2.0 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Profile Sync Gate (Phase 0B)** | No cross-platform audit | Mandatory sync check across LinkedIn, Dice, Indeed before any rebuild begins. Date/title mismatches caught before they reach a recruiter. |
| 2 | **Director Disqualifier Scan** | No disqualifier filter | Fires before every resume output. Each flagged entry (retail roles, gap-fillers, junior titles) gets REMOVE / REFRAME / KEEP verdict. Nothing ships without candidate confirmation. |
| 3 | **Technical Platform Optimization (Phase 1C)** | LinkedIn only | Dedicated lane for Dice and Indeed: preferred title rewrite, top 5 skills reorder for AI/Director signal, education field corrections, salary floor calibration. |
| 4 | **Date/Title Verification Prompt** | Not present | Hardcoded into Phase 1. Agent asks candidate to confirm all dates, titles, and company names match across every active profile before the resume is finalized. |
| 5 | **Phase 2 Web Fallback** | Apify MCP required | Apify preferred but no longer required. Falls back to web search sweep across LinkedIn Jobs, Dice, and Indeed when Apify is not connected. |
| 6 | **Cover Letter Mode (Phase 3)** | One cover sentence only | Optional Director-register cover letter: 3 paragraphs, under 300 words, with structural rules enforced. Activates on request. |
| 7 | **Offer Intelligence & Negotiation (Phase 5)** | Framework ended at network activation | Full offer lifecycle: market comp analysis, negotiation strategy, counter language (email + verbal), acceptance or decline drafts. |

## Six-Phase Architecture

```
Phase 0   →   Phase 0B   →   Phase 1   →   Phase 2   →   Phase 3   →   Phase 4   →   Phase 5
Identity      Profile        Profile        Target         Per-App        Network        Offer
Calibration   Sync Audit     Dominance      Acquisition    Tailoring      Activation     Negotiation
```

### Phase 0 -- Identity Calibration Intake

[![Input](https://img.shields.io/badge/Input-Resume_%2B_LinkedIn_PDF-374151?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)

Builds the **Candidate Intelligence Brief** across five components:

- **A. Positioning Tier** -- Level presenting vs. level targeting. Honest compensation gap analysis.
- **B. Identity Signal Audit** -- What does each document say in 6 seconds? Are they consistent?
- **C. Market Reality Check** -- External hire rates, search firm vs. direct-apply strategy, top 3 self-sabotage patterns at this level.
- **D. Competitive Positioning** -- Shortlisted candidate profile vs. this candidate. No softening.
- **E. Search Strategy** -- 10-15 tiered target companies, 2-3 overlooked adjacent roles, daily cadence.

### Phase 0B -- Profile Sync Audit *(NEW in v2.0)*

[![Input](https://img.shields.io/badge/Input-Phase_0_Brief_%2B_Profile_URLs-374151?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)
[![Gate](https://img.shields.io/badge/Gate-MANDATORY_before_Phase_1-darkred?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)

Misaligned profiles kill Director-level candidacies at the verification stage. This phase cross-checks every active platform against the resume and produces a sync gap table:

| Platform | Field | Resume Value | Profile Value | Action Required |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Employment dates | 2022-2024 | 2021-2023 | Correct LinkedIn |
| Dice | Preferred title | Director, AI Architecture | sr solutions architect, gen ai, startups... | Rewrite immediately |
| Dice | Top skill | Artificial Intelligence | Management | Reorder skills |

Candidate must confirm all gaps resolved before Phase 1 proceeds.

### Phase 1 -- Profile Dominance

[![Input](https://img.shields.io/badge/Input-Phase_0B_Confirmed_Brief-374151?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)

**Director Disqualifier Scan fires first** -- before any output is produced.

- **1A. LinkedIn Rebuild** -- Boolean recruiter simulation, 3 headline options, full About section draft, top 3 Experience rewrites, skills audit.
- **1B. Master Resume** -- Executive register rebuild from scratch. Scope lines, P&L framing, outcome bullets with numbers. No generic language.
- **1C. Technical Platform Optimization** *(NEW)* -- Dice and Indeed optimization: title, skills reorder, education corrections, salary floor.
- **Date/Title Verification Prompt** *(NEW)* -- Mandatory confirmation step before any resume is finalized.

### Phase 2 -- Target Acquisition (Daily Sweep)

[![Input](https://img.shields.io/badge/Input-Phase_0_Brief_%2B_Target_Role-374151?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)
[![Primary](https://img.shields.io/badge/Primary-Apify_MCP-2563EB?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)
[![Fallback](https://img.shields.io/badge/Fallback-Web_Search_Sweep-228B22?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)

Daily executive job sweep with Director Filter applied to every result:
- Internal fill risk assessment
- Company health flags (layoffs, freeze, restructuring)
- Urgency signal (budget-approved vs. pipeline-building)
- Realistic compensation ceiling check

Output: Ranked table + top 5 to apply today + top 3 for direct HM outreach + roles to skip with reasoning.

---

### Phase 3 -- Per-Application ATS + Executive Tailoring

[![Input](https://img.shields.io/badge/Input-Master_Resume_%2B_Full_JD-374151?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)
[![Run](https://img.shields.io/badge/Cadence-Once_Per_Application-6B7280?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)

Tailors for two simultaneous audiences:

**ATS:** Keyword extraction (required vs. preferred), before/after match score, natural gap rewrite.

**Hiring Manager:** Three shortlist questions answered -- Has this person done this at scale? Can I defend this hire? Will they make my team better?

Deliverables: ATS score, tailored resume, HM confidence score (0-100), one sharp cover sentence, red flags list.

**Cover Letter Mode** *(NEW)* -- Activates on request. 3-paragraph Director-register letter, under 300 words.

---

### Phase 4 -- Executive Network Activation

[![Input](https://img.shields.io/badge/Input-Phase_0_Brief_%2B_Connections.csv-374151?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)

Four steps: Connection mapping (1st-degree + warm 2nd-degree paths), Director-level outreach rules, personalized message generation with 5-day follow-up, recruiter outreach to retained and contingency search firms.

Rule: At $120K+, the message reads like a peer initiating a relevant professional conversation -- not a job seeker asking for help.

---

### Phase 5 -- Offer Intelligence & Negotiation Protocol *(NEW in v2.0)*

[![Input](https://img.shields.io/badge/Input-Offer_Letter_or_Verbal_Details-374151?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)

Four steps:
1. **Offer Intelligence** -- Market comp analysis, total comp breakdown, negotiation leverage identification.
2. **Negotiation Strategy** -- Accept / Counter / Walk scoring with clear reasoning. Specific ask, not range.
3. **Counter Language** -- Email counter (under 150 words), verbal counter (under 60 seconds), pushback response.
4. **Acceptance or Walk** -- Formal acceptance email or professional decline that preserves the relationship.

## Output Standards

[![Standard](https://img.shields.io/badge/No-Em_Dashes-darkred?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)
[![Standard](https://img.shields.io/badge/No-Junior_Register_Language-darkred?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)
[![Standard](https://img.shields.io/badge/Bullets-Action_%2B_Scale_%2B_Outcome-2563EB?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)
[![Standard](https://img.shields.io/badge/Outreach-Peer_Tone_%2B_Under_75_Words-2563EB?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)
[![Standard](https://img.shields.io/badge/Assessments-Honest_First_Constructive_Second-228B22?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/emcdo411/elite-jobseeker-agent-)

- All resume bullets: `[Action verb] + [what you did] + [at what scale] + [with what outcome]`
- All outreach: specific, peer-tone, under 75 words for first contact
- All assessments: honest first, constructive second -- gaps are not softened
