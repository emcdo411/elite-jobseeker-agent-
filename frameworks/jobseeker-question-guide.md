## How This Works

Each question below is designed to fire a specific phase of the agent. Ask them in order and the full five-phase workflow runs from intake to network activation. You can also jump to any single phase by asking that phase's question directly.

Every question assumes you have already uploaded your resume PDF and LinkedIn PDF unless noted otherwise.

---

## The Master Launch Question
*Ask this first. Fires Phase 0 immediately and sets the foundation for every phase that follows.*

> "I am a senior professional targeting Director-level roles at $120K or above. I have uploaded my resume and LinkedIn profile. Build my Candidate Intelligence Brief and tell me exactly where I stand in the market right now."

**What fires:** Full Phase 0 -- Positioning Tier, Identity Signal Audit, Market Reality Check, Competitive Positioning, and Search Strategy. The agent will output all five components and wait for your confirmation before moving to Phase 1.

---

## Phase 0 -- Identity Calibration

### Primary trigger
> "Run Phase 0. Here is my resume and LinkedIn PDF. Tell me what a recruiter concludes about me in six seconds and where the gaps are."

### Alternate triggers
> "Build my Candidate Intelligence Brief."

> "What does the market actually think of me right now based on these documents?"

> "Run the career intelligence intake. I want the honest version, not the encouraging version."

> "Compare where I am presenting versus where I am targeting. Be direct."

> "What are the top three ways I am self-sabotaging my own job search at this level?"

**What you need uploaded:** Resume PDF + LinkedIn PDF

**What fires:** Five-component intelligence brief covering your positioning tier, document consistency, market reality, competitive gap, and a tiered target company list with search strategy.

## Phase 1 -- Profile Dominance

### Primary trigger
> "Run Phase 1. Rebuild my LinkedIn and my master resume from the recruiter's screen, not mine."

### Alternate triggers
> "Rewrite my LinkedIn headline, About section, and top three experience bullet sets for a $120K Director-level search."

> "Run the resume rebuild. Use executive register. I want scope lines, P&L framing, and outcome bullets with numbers."

> "Simulate a Boolean recruiter search for my target role and tell me if I would appear. Then fix the gaps."

> "Audit my LinkedIn for everything that is actively hurting me at this level."

> "Write my resume summary block. I want a thesis statement and three proof points. No generic language."

> "Run the executive language audit. Flag every junior-register phrase in my resume and replace it."

**What you need:** Phase 0 Candidate Intelligence Brief completed and confirmed

**What fires:** Full LinkedIn rebuild with three headline options, About section draft, and top experience rewrites. Master resume rebuilt from scratch with executive register, summary block, and one-page vs. two-page ruling.

---

## Phase 2 -- Target Acquisition

### Primary trigger
> "Run Phase 2. My target role today is [role title]. Sweep for Director-level openings posted in the last seven days at companies with 200 to 10,000 employees paying $120K or above."

### Alternate triggers
> "Run the daily job sweep for [role title] and apply the Director Filter to every result."

> "Find me the top five roles to apply to today and the top three companies to reach out to directly. Skip anything that is a contract, temp, or agency post."

> "Score each result for internal fill risk, company health, and urgency signal. Rank them."

> "Which of these openings are actually hiring versus just pipeline building? Flag the difference."

> "Run the executive job sweep and tell me which roles to skip and exactly why."

**What you need:** Phase 0 brief confirmed + target role title provided + Apify MCP connected (or web search fallback)

**What fires:** Ranked results table with Director Filter applied to every result. Top five to apply today. Top three for direct hiring manager outreach. Full skip list with reasoning.

---

## Phase 3 -- Per-Application ATS + Executive Tailoring

### Primary trigger
> "Run Phase 3. Here is the job description. Tailor my master resume for both the ATS system and the hiring manager. Give me the before and after ATS score, the tailored resume ready to submit, and the hiring manager confidence score."

### Alternate triggers
> "Score my resume against this job description. Tell me the keyword gaps and rewrite the bullets that are not landing."

> "What three questions is the hiring manager asking before they shortlist someone for this role? Then show me if my resume answers them."

> "Run the ATS tailoring. No keyword stuffing. Natural integration only."

> "What are the red flags I need to remove from my resume before I submit to this specific role?"

> "Write me the one cover sentence for this application. Not a paragraph. One sentence."

> "Give me the hiring manager confidence score for this submission and tell me specifically why it is not higher."

**What you need:** Phase 1B master resume + full job description pasted as text

**What fires:** ATS keyword extraction and gap analysis. Before and after match score. Fully tailored resume ready to submit. Hiring manager confidence score with specific reasoning. One cover sentence. Red flag removal list.

## Phase 4 -- Executive Network Activation

### Primary trigger
> "Run Phase 4. Here is my LinkedIn connections CSV. Map every connection I have at my target companies and generate peer-level outreach for the priority contacts."

### Alternate triggers
> "Who do I know at [company name] and what is the best way to reach them without sounding like I am asking for a job?"

> "Map my second-degree paths into [company name]. Who do I know who knows someone there?"

> "Draft a Director-level outreach message to [contact name] at [company]. Under 75 words. Peer tone. Goal is a 20-minute call, not a job."

> "Write a follow-up message for a contact who did not respond after five business days. Keep it clean."

> "Identify the retained and contingency search firms that place at $120K+ in [sector]. Then draft my outreach to a senior search partner."

> "What are the three things most candidates say in recruiter outreach that kill the conversation? Then show me what to say instead."

**What you need:** Phase 0 brief confirmed + LinkedIn connections export CSV uploaded (Settings > Data Privacy > Get a copy of your data > Connections.csv)

**What fires:** Full connection map for each target company. Priority contact list with title and tenure. Warm path mapping through second-degree connections. Personalized outreach drafts for each priority contact. Five-day follow-up messages. Recruiter outreach sequence with search firm list.

## Full Sequential Run -- Copy and Use

If you want to run all five phases start to finish in one session, ask these five questions in order. Wait for confirmation at the end of each phase before sending the next.

```
1. "I am targeting Director-level roles at $120K or above. I have uploaded my resume and LinkedIn PDF. Build my Candidate Intelligence Brief and tell me exactly where I stand in the market right now."

2. "Phase 0 confirmed. Run Phase 1. Rebuild my LinkedIn and master resume from the recruiter's screen."

3. "Phase 1 confirmed. Run Phase 2. My target role today is [role title]. Apply the Director Filter to every result."

4. "Phase 2 confirmed. Run Phase 3 for this job description: [paste full JD]. Give me the tailored resume, ATS score, and hiring manager confidence score."

5. "Phase 3 confirmed. Run Phase 4. Here is my LinkedIn connections CSV. Map my target companies and generate outreach for the priority contacts."
```

## What the Agent Will Never Do

Knowing what not to ask is as useful as knowing what to ask.

- It will not soften a gap assessment. If your resume is not competitive at this level it will say so directly.
- It will not generate encouragement language. "You have a lot to offer" is not intelligence.
- It will not skip a phase because you are in a hurry. Phases depend on each other. Phase 3 without Phase 1 produces weak output.
- It will not write junior-register language in any draft. Words like "passionate about," "team player," and "results-oriented" do not appear in the output.
- It will not produce vague outreach. Every message has a specific hook, a specific ask, and a word count under 75 for first contact.
