01 // Scoring Model

SEVEN SIGNALS THAT CHANGE THE CONVERSATION.

The tool moves the job search away from generic resume advice and into a consulting-style evaluation model: identity, market tier, compensation, stakeholder influence, transformation leadership, executive readiness, and hiring manager confidence.

01Identity Calibration Score

Measures whether the profile signals a coherent senior operator identity or reads like scattered experience.

02Market Positioning Tier

Classifies the candidate as Manager, Senior Manager, Director, or stretch-level executive signal.

03Compensation Alignment

Tests whether the evidence supports the desired salary band and where the stretch range begins.

04Stakeholder Influence

Evaluates C-suite exposure, executive communication, cross-functional leadership, and trust signal.

05Transformation Leadership

Scores change management, implementation ownership, AI adoption, governance, and delivery credibility.

06Executive Readiness

Assesses whether the candidate sounds ready to sit in the room, frame ambiguity, and advise leaders.

07Hiring Manager Confidence

The final decision signal: would a leader trust this candidate with an ambiguous, high-impact engagement?
