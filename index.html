03 // Intelligence Signals

WHAT THIS CHECKS THAT GENERIC TOOLS MISS.

Above $120K, the search changes. Recruiters look for credibility signals, buyer language, scope, judgment, proof, and whether the candidate sounds like someone who can sit in the room.

Are you presenting like a $120K+ operator, or accidentally framing yourself like a task executor?
Does your resume show P&L, risk, scale, systems, stakeholder translation, and decision support?
Are you targeting roles that are genuinely external-hire viable, or roles likely reserved for internal succession?
Does your outreach sound like a peer-to-peer operator or like a candidate begging for consideration?
Are you using company signals, funding, product shifts, and hiring motion to prioritize opportunities?
Does your positioning translate technical fluency into business value instead of drowning in tools?
